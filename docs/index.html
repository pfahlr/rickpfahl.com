About Rick Pfahl

I'm a senior-level web developer, systems architect, and technology problem-solver with over 15 years of experience building, securing, and optimizing digital systems. My career has taken me from large-scale marketing sites and complex CMS deployments to highly specialized automation, data analysis, and security projects for businesses, investors, and individuals.
I'm fluent in a wide range of programming languages and frameworks — PHP, Python, JavaScript, Node.js, React, Symfony, Flask, MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB — and equally at home working on Linux servers, tuning SEO, building DevOps pipelines, or implementing cybersecurity best practices. Over the years, I've also built expertise in OSINT, automation, and competitive intelligence, giving me a unique ability to approach challenges from a strategic, data-driven perspective.
As the founder of New Literacy Media Group, I've delivered high-performance websites and SEO strategies for small and medium businesses. With TaxDeedReports.com, I developed a proprietary data-scraping system that transforms public records into actionable real estate intelligence. My work blends deep technical knowledge with practical problem-solving, whether I'm building something new, improving something that already exists, or uncovering opportunities others miss.
Beyond my professional work, I've been an organizer, teacher, and advocate, applying the same skills I use in technology to grassroots community projects. I believe in clear communication, precision in execution, and creating systems — whether for code, data, or people — that actually work.
If you've got a challenge that demands technical expertise, creative thinking, and a relentless focus on results, I'm the person who gets it done.
